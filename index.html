The questions Texas homeowners ask us most.

Do you offer same-day appliance repair in Houston?

Yes — in most cases we match you with a local technician for same-day or next-day service across Houston and surrounding Texas cities.

How much does appliance repair cost in Houston?

Most repairs start with a service-call diagnostic, which is often waived when you proceed with the repair. Final cost depends on the appliance and parts, and you always get an upfront quote before any work begins.

Which appliances and brands do you cover?

Refrigerators, washers, dryers, dishwashers, ovens, ranges, stoves, microwaves and freezers — all major brands including Samsung, LG, Whirlpool, GE, Bosch and Sub-Zero.

Are the pros licensed and insured?

Yes. Every pro in the Zbroo network is vetted, licensed, insured and background-checked.

What areas in Texas do you serve?

Houston and the greater metro: Sugar Land, Katy, Cypress, Spring, The Woodlands, Pearland, Pasadena, Conroe and nearby communities.

Is there a warranty on repairs?

Yes — repairs come with a labor warranty, typically 90 days, plus any applicable manufacturer parts warranty.
